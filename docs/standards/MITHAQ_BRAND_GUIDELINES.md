# MITHAQ Brand Guidelines
**Status: FINAL | Phase 1 Enforcement**

MITHAQ relies on a forensic, non-SaaS visual identity to project absolute neutrality, mathematical proof, and intellectual authority in the Enterprise B2B and critical infrastructure sectors.

## 1. The Core Palette
Gradients exceeding a 15° rotation are strictly prohibited. These specific HEX values are locked.

* **Obsidian Black (`#0A0A0A`):** The primary background. Represents depth, the void, and air-gapped execution.
* **Cobalt Steel (`#4682B4`):** The primary accent and **DAREE3** identity. Represents cold precision, structural integrity, and defense.
* **Bismuth Purple (`#8E44AD`):** The secondary accent and **AI Swarm** identity. Represents AI cognition and orchestration.
* **Cryptographic White (`#FFFFFF`):** Text, keylines, and **AL-BURHAN** clarity. Represents transparency and verifiable truth.

## 2. Typography
* **Primary Latin (Headers/UI):** `Rubik` (Bold for masterbrand headers, Medium for module names).
* **Secondary Latin (Body/Compliance):** `Work Sans` (Light weight only).
* **Primary Arabic (Headers):** `Kufi One`.
* **Secondary Arabic (Body):** `Amiri`.
* *Kerning Rule:* Track "MITHAQ" headers at `-50`. No serif fonts permitted outside of Arabic body copy.

## 3. UI/UX Rules
Logos and UI components must embrace extreme geometric reductionism:
* Maximum of 8 vector paths per icon.
* No curves with a radius greater than 45°.
* Strict 3:1 negative space ratio.
* **Do Not:** Use drop shadows, glows, or RGB outside the approved palette.
