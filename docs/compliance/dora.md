# DORA

CHERENKOV maps findings to the **EU Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA)** requirements.

## Mapped Categories

| DORA Requirement | CHERENKOV Coverage |
|---|---|
| **ICT Risk Management** | Full — AI-driven vulnerability detection |
| **ICT Incident Reporting** | Full — signed trace audit trail |
| **Digital Operational Resilience Testing** | Full — continuous validation |
| **Third-Party Risk** | Full — wrapper-based assessments |
| **Information Sharing** | Partial — anonymized telemetry |

## Evidence

CHERENKOV traces serve as DORA-compliant evidence artifacts, providing cryptographic proof of testing and validation activities.
