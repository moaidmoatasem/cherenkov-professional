# What is CHERENKOV?

CHERENKOV is an air-gapped, sovereign AI security testing platform. It finds vulnerabilities, proves them with cryptographic evidence, and learns from every scan — without your data ever leaving your hardware.

## The Three Pillars

| Pillar | What it does |
|---|---|
| **MEISSNER** | Enforces zero-egress network isolation |
| **ABLATION** | Redacts PII before any data leaves the host |
| **TOKAMAK** | Executes live, non-destructive Proofs of Concept |

## Why CHERENKOV?

- **Sovereign** — Runs entirely on your hardware. No data egress.
- **Provable** — Every finding includes a cryptographically signed PoC.
- **Adaptive** — Learns from every scan via the LATTICE memory system.
- **Compliant** — Maps findings to EGY-FIN CSF, SAMA CSF, and DORA.
